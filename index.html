School Achievements

Sports
Sporting Activities

Sports and Games encompass more than just the benefits of physical things but also increase confidence, self esteem and mental alertness. Every student of Meridian exhibits true sportsmanship which is very essential to deal with life’s challenges. We proudly boast of our expert coaches and trainers who trained the students to win various competitions at Nationals. We offer various sports such as Cricket, Basket Ball, Throw Ball, Table Tennis, Chess, Yoga, Taekwondo.

Creative Activities
Creative Activities

Being creative helps you become a better problem solver. Creative people are better abled to live with uncertainty. Meridian provides the right platform to hone the creative skills among the students. Performing Arts play a significant role in the co-curricular activities of the school. State of art infrastructure gives the right impetus to develop creative skills. Students are given various opportunities through regular assemblies, special celebrations, competitions etc. Our school Annual Days are always the best productions in the twin cities. Some of our creative initiatives are: Under The Mango Tree – Reading Club International Dimension in Schools – A British Council Programme Confero – Debating Club Melange – Interschool Literary and Cultural Fest Music Club – In association with Furtados School of Music Moot Court, MUNs, Sculpture, Heritage Club, Eco Club, Rotary Interact Club, Filmit, Theatre, Western and Classical Music and Dance, Painting, Photography and many more.

Meridian MUN 2020
Meridian MUN 2020

Meridian School Banjara Hills hosted its Fifth Edition of Model United Nations, Meridian MUN 2020 on 30th & 31st October 2020. But this time with a difference ! Keeping the current situation in mind the sessions were organised on a virtual platform but without losing its originality and gravity. Along with the two-day conference with extremely talented and proficient Executive Boards, ROP (Rules of Procedure) Training Sessions for the delegates were organised, and a Career fair by some of the top universities in the world to provide exposure to the delegates and equip them with the necessary skill sets they may need during the conference and their future endeavours as well.
